As-skitter Dataset

The NetScience dataset represents the coauthorship network of scientists working in network science. It captures collaborations between researchers who have coauthored scientific papers together. The network was extracted from papers listed in M. E. J. Newman’s 2006 review article on network theory, and reflects professional collaborations within the field of complex networks. The dataset includes:

  • 1,589 scientists (nodes) who have authored papers in the field.
  • 2,742 coauthorship relations (edges) representing joint publications.
  • Some papers have multiple authors; therefore, a group of n authors creates a complete clique of n(n−1)/2 edges in the network.
  • The network is undirected, meaning that a coauthorship between two scientists is treated symmetrically.
