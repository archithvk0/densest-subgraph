Ca-Hep Dataset

The ca-HepTh dataset represents the collaboration network of authors of scientific papers submitted to the High Energy Physics Theory (HEP-TH) category on arXiv, an open-access repository for scientific papers. In this network, each node corresponds to a scientist, and an undirected edge between two nodes indicates that the two authors coauthored at least one paper together between January 1993 and April 2003. The network includes:

  • 9,877 authors (nodes).
  • 51,971 collaboration links (edges), where an edge signifies that two authors have jointly published one or more papers.
  • The network is undirected, as coauthorship is a mutual relationship.
